Masala Chai

Ingredients: Black tea leaves, Plant-based milk, Water, Spices (cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves), Sweetener (sugar or agave syrup),

INSTRUCTIONS: -

1. Boil water and add black tea leaves, spices, and sweetener.

2.Simmer, add plant-based milk, and strain.
