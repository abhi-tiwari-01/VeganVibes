Tofu Tikka

Tofu Tikka is a flavourful appetizer that is a change to your usual paneer tikka recipe. The tofu is marinated with a mixture of curd, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala and red chilli powder to give it that spicy twist. This tikka makes for a wonderful appetizer to be served if you are planning to have your friends over for a movie night. This Tofu tikka is also a treat for those who do not like paneer much and prefer tofu.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

1. Marinate tofu cubes in a mixture of vegan yogurt, tandoori masala, lemon juice, garlic, and ginger paste.

step 2

2. Thread marinated tofu onto skewers and grill until charred.

step 3

3. Serve with mint chutney.

ingredients

Extra-firm tofu, cubed

Vegan yogurt

Tandoori masala spice mix

Lemon juice

Garlic and ginger paste

Skewers

tools

Pan

Gas Stove

Measuring Cups
